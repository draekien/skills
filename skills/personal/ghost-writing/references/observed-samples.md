# Calibration samples

A minimal ground-truth set, one or two samples per register, distilled June 2026 from AI-session transcripts, chat history, and sent email. Extracts are anonymised: personal names, product names, and identifying details are replaced with placeholders without altering sentence structure, rhythm, or word choice. Use for comparison when a draft's authenticity is in doubt; the principles in the skill body are the primary instruction.
