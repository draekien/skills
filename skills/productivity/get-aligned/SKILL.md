---
name: get-aligned
description: 'Align human+AI before acting. Use when: ambiguity, multiple valid approaches, unstated constraints, non-trivial scope — or human says "help me think through", "let''s figure out", "work with me on", or asks for plan/design without full requirements.'
---

Interview relentlessly about every aspect of request until we reach shared understanding. Identify the highest-impact open decision first. Ask about it, then use the answer to surface the next dependent decision. Continue until no unknowns remain. For contested or consequential decisions, briefly state the trade-offs and your recommendation. For clear or low-stakes questions, ask directly without analysis.

Ask questions one at a time.

If question answerable by exploring project, explore project instead.

If the human asks you to proceed before all open questions are resolved, list any remaining unknowns briefly, state the assumptions you will use, and begin.
